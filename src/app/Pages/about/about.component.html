Dr. Hany Abdel-Gawad Suleiman is one of the most prominent spine surgeons in Egypt and worldwide, specializing in spine surgery for both children and adults. His interest in this field began in 2004 during his orthopedic residency at Al-Azhar University, where he worked in the spine unit for three years, aspiring to become one of the best spine surgeons globally. After years of hard work, his dream came true.
